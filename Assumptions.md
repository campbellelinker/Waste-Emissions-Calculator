# Landfill Carbon Emissions

## Waste weight
- One gallon of mixed paper weighs 1.359375 lbs ([source](https://www.montgomerycountymd.gov/department-environmental-protection/recycling-impact-calculator))
- One cubic yard of compostable food waste weighs 463 lbs ([source](https://www.epa.gov/sites/default/files/2016-04/documents/volume_to_weight_conversion_factors_memorandum_04192016_508fnl.pdf))
- One cubic yard of recyclable containers (plastic bottles, aluminum cans, steel cans, glass bottles) weighs 180 lbs ([source](https://www.epa.gov/sites/default/files/2016-04/documents/volume_to_weight_conversion_factors_memorandum_04192016_508fnl.pdf))
- One cubic yard of uncompacted municipal solid waste weighs 275 lbs ([source](https://www.epa.gov/sites/default/files/2016-04/documents/volume_to_weight_conversion_factors_memorandum_04192016_508fnl.pdf))

## Waste emissions
- 0.11 tons CO₂e per ton food waste, composted ([source - Table 9](https://www.epa.gov/system/files/documents/2025-01/ghg-emission-factors-hub-2025.pdf))
- 902 kg CO2e per ton municipal solid waste ([source](https://www.epa.gov/system/files/documents/2025-01/ghg-emission-factors-hub-2025.pdf))
- 0.07 tons CO2e per ton primarily residential mixed paper ([source - Table 9](https://www.epa.gov/system/files/documents/2025-01/ghg-emission-factors-hub-2025.pdf))
- 0.09 tons CO2e per ton mixed recyclables ([source - Table 9](https://www.epa.gov/system/files/documents/2025-01/ghg-emission-factors-hub-2025.pdf))

## Waste compaction
- 4:1 ratio of uncompacted to compacted trash ([source](https://thebusinessblog.org/rear-loader-garbage-truck-specifications-explained/), [source](https://www.sciencing.com/calculate-compaction-ratio-7556751/), [source](https://capcoa.org/))

# Transportation Carbon Emissions

## Truck attributes
- If Pedal People used garbage trucks instead of bikes to collect waste, they would use automated side loader (ASL) trucks fueled with compressed natural gas ([source](https://www.usarecycle.com/news/asl-truck))
- The capacity of an ASL truck is 28 cubic yards ([source](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/10.3141/2233-10))

## Truck emissions
- These trucks emit 17790.90909 (grams/mile) on average ([source](https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/epdf/10.3141/2233-10), [source](https://ww2.arb.ca.gov/sites/default/files/2020-06/gappc.pdf), [source](https://capcoa.org/))
- Individual transportation emissions can be estimated by dividing the total emissions of a truck driving a route by the number of pickups on that route

## Truck route
- The distance to travel from A to B is comparable to the distance to travel from B to A in our route calculations. The impact of one-way streets is negligible. However, this assumption may not be satisfied for all scenarios. 

## Contextualizing information
- To drive from Northampton to select destinations, the average car runs on unleaded fuel, has a fuel efficiency of 25 mpg, and emits roughly 0.78 lbs CO2 per mile (source)
